Equipment

The things you need are

  1. Lacrosse Stick

    2. There are differet types of sticks you can get one with a mesh back or one without. The specifics of the stick don't really matter as long as you're comfortable with it and can play with it.

    Field Player Stick Goalie stick
  2. Mouthguard

    3. All you really need is a top mouthguard. The thinner the better because it'll make field communication easier and it provides better comfort.

    Sisu Mouthguard
  3. Goggles or Helmet

    4. Goggles are more commonly used when playing lacrosse but helmets are also sometimes used.

    Goggles Helmet
  4. Cleats or turf shoes(depending on where you will play)
